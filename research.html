Research

Research Interests

  • My research interests lie in the fields of Computer Vision, Multimedia, Natural Language Processing, and Machine Learning. I have worked on hand, gesture, human pose, multi-modal joint understanding, image and video captioning, object detection, action detection, human ReID, shape understanding, reinforcement learning, adversarial machine learning, etc.

  • My current research focuses include: 1) human pose, hand, and gesture, 2) object detection, action detection, and human Re-ID, 3) multi-modal joint understanding, vision and language.
